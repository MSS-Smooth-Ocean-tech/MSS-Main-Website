Our Story

Operational support shaped by maritime experience

Maritime Support Solutions (MSS) was founded in 2022 by a team of shipping professionals who saw a recurring challenge across the industry: strong shipping and logistics businesses slowed down by operational complexity.

In an industry still reliant on manual documentation, fragmented systems, and overstretched teams, MSS offers a smarter, more controlled way forward. Backed by over 65 years of combined maritime experience, we help leaders streamline operations for scale, speed, and accuracy through a focused blend of domain expertise, workflow automation, and high-accuracy business process outsourcing without disrupting existing workflows.

Registered in Kannur, Kerala, India, MSS is powered by a skilled team managing high-volume shipping documentation, back-office processes, and compliance-driven workflows with consistency and precision.
