The Challenge: Bottlenecks & Delays

Before engaging with MSS Maritime, the client — a major NVOCC operator in the APAC region — struggled with manual data entry processes that were prone to human error. Documentation for shipping manifests often took up to two full business days to process, leading to demurrage charges and customer dissatisfaction.

Key Pain Points:
Excessive manual data entry causing a 12% error rate.
Documentation turnaround averaging 48 hours, delaying vessel departures.
Rising operational costs due to overtime pay for administrative staff.

The MSS Solution

We implemented a hybrid solution combining our expert maritime BPO team with custom Optical Character Recognition (OCR) workflows. This approach allowed for rapid digitization of physical manifests while maintaining human-in-the-loop verification for quality assurance.

Automated OCR Capture

Deployed intelligent document processing to extract data from BOLs automatically.

24/7 QA Teams

Dedicated maritime specialists reviewing exceptions in real-time across time zones.
